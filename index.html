Purpose of MyTePapa

The purpose of MyTePapa is to provide an engaging and interactive experience for Te Papa Visitors through a simplistic app that will be available on tablets for visitors to rent out.

MyTePapa implements a chat system, allowing visitors to chat to Te Papa staff member at any time for help, directions or more information about an exhibit.

MyTePapa integrates and external database to enable photo recognition with the camera implementation. Layar can be used to connect to augmented material about a particular exhibit.

Photo Recognition API

The implementation of the recognize.im API users can scan images in the museum and connect to the ID I gave it in my database.

This can be used for navigating from one level of Te Papa to another and to connect to further information about an exhibit.
