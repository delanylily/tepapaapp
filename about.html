Activating camera for hiiii photo recognition

MyTePapa activates the device's camera for the user to scan an image and connect to the image recognition API.

The camera connects automatically when the page is loaded for simplicity of the app's purpose. This camera can also be used with Layar to scan exhibits and connect with augmented

content relating to the exhibit.

When taking a photo for recognition, the user simply captures the image with the device which will connect to an image ID from the database. This app is called Recognize.im
